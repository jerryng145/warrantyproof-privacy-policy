## Full Description

WarrantyProof: Receipts is a local-first iOS app for organizing proof of purchase and warranty records. It is designed for electronics, appliances, tools, devices, furniture, and other purchases where receipts, serial numbers, warranty dates, manuals, and support notes may matter later.

Unlike a spreadsheet or photo album, WarrantyProof is built around product records and claim-ready proof. It is not an accounting app, tax tool, insurance product, or guarantee of claim approval.

## FAQ For Product Hunt

### Is WarrantyProof an OCR receipt scanner?

WarrantyProof should be positioned as a receipt and warranty organizer, not a full accounting OCR platform unless OCR is explicitly available in the current app.

### Is WarrantyProof an accounting app?

No. It is not for tax filing, business accounting, or expense reports. It is for organizing proof of purchase and warranty records.

### Does WarrantyProof guarantee warranty claims?

No. Retailers, manufacturers, service providers, and insurers make claim decisions. WarrantyProof helps users keep relevant proof easier to find.

### Why local-first?

Receipts, serial numbers, and product records can be private. Local-first storage makes the product easier to understand and safer to recommend for users who do not want automatic server uploads.
