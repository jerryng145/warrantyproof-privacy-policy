FAQ

What is a warranty tracker?

A warranty tracker keeps product purchase proof, warranty dates, serial numbers, retailer details, and claim notes together so the information is easier to find before a repair, refund, or warranty request.

What proof should I keep for warranty claims?

Useful proof includes the receipt, order confirmation, purchase date, retailer name, serial number, warranty length, product photos, manuals, and notes from any support conversation.

Is WarrantyProof a receipt scanner?

WarrantyProof is primarily a local-first proof organizer. It helps keep receipt and warranty records organized, but it should not be described as an accounting OCR platform unless that feature is explicitly available in the app.

Does WarrantyProof guarantee claim approval?

No. WarrantyProof helps organize proof and reminders, but claim, refund, repair, or insurance decisions are made by the retailer, manufacturer, service provider, or insurer.

Who is WarrantyProof best for?

WarrantyProof is best for people who want one private place to keep receipts, warranty dates, serial numbers, retailer details, manuals, and claim notes for electronics, appliances, tools, and other important purchases.
