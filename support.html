FAQ

Does WarrantyProof require an account?

No. WarrantyProof is designed as a local-first app with no account system.

Where are my receipts and proof documents stored?

They are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Does the app upload my receipts automatically?

No. WarrantyProof does not automatically upload receipts, warranty files, serial numbers, or notes to a server.

How do reminders work?

Warranty reminders use local notifications on your device. The app asks for notification permission only when reminders are enabled.

Who handles Premium purchases?

Premium purchases and subscriptions are handled by the app store where you installed WarrantyProof. On Android, purchases use Google Play Billing. On iPhone and iPad, purchases use Apple's in-app purchase system.

Does WarrantyProof guarantee a warranty claim, refund, repair, or insurance outcome?

No. WarrantyProof helps organize your proof, but approval decisions are made by the retailer, manufacturer, service provider, or other party handling your request.
