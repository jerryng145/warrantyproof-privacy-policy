What is a receipt and warranty tracker?

A receipt and warranty tracker is a dedicated place to keep proof of purchase, product details, warranty dates, serial numbers, retailer notes, product photos, PDFs, and claim notes. The goal is to make key details easier to find before a repair, refund, replacement, or warranty request.

Why spreadsheets fail for warranty claims

A spreadsheet can hold a purchase date and a warranty expiry date, but the real claim record usually needs more than two cells. The receipt image may be in Photos, the order confirmation in email, the manual in Downloads, the serial number on the item, and the claim notes in a message thread. When a product breaks, that split record becomes slow to reconstruct.

FAQ

Is WarrantyProof better than a spreadsheet?

It depends on the workflow. A spreadsheet can work for simple lists. WarrantyProof is better suited when you want receipts, warranty dates, serial numbers, photos, documents, and claim notes attached to product records.

Can WarrantyProof store proof of purchase?

WarrantyProof is designed to help organize proof of purchase and claim-ready product records. Users should still verify saved details before relying on them for support, refund, warranty, or insurance requests.

Does WarrantyProof upload receipts to a server?

The public privacy positioning is local-first: no account, no ads, no tracking, and no app-owned server upload. Records stay on device unless the user exports or shares them.

Does WarrantyProof guarantee a warranty claim?

No. WarrantyProof helps organize proof and reminders, but claim decisions are made by the retailer, manufacturer, service provider, or insurer.
